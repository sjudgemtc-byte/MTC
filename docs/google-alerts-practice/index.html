Set Up a Google Alert

A Google Alert sends email updates when Google finds new results for a topic you choose. It can help you follow jobs, weather, local news, study topics, scams, hobbies, or workplace information.

Learning goals

Open Google Alerts, choose a safe topic, create an alert, and explain what the alert will send.

What you need

A browser, internet access, and a Google account if you want to save an alert.

Trainer note

Students may observe the demonstration instead of signing in. Do not require students to show their personal email inbox.
