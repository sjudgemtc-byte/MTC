Set Up a Google Alert

Google Alerts can send email updates when Google finds new results for a topic you choose.

Learning goals

Open Google Alerts, choose a safe topic, create an alert, and explain what the alert will send.

Safe topics

Try Redfern community news, online safety Australia, or entry level warehouse jobs Sydney.

Open Google Alerts

Privacy rule

Do not create alerts using your full name, address, phone number, passport, Medicare, banking details, health details, or passwords.
