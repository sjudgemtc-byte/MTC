Finding a Creative Commons Image

Use Google Image Search to find a Grand Canyon image, filter by Creative Commons licences, check the original source, and write a simple image credit.

Activity Steps

Key idea: Google helps us find images, but Google does not own the images. Always check the source and licence.
20-30 minutes

Purpose of the Task

In this activity, you will learn how to search for an image online and check whether it has a Creative Commons licence. The goal is not just to find a nice picture. The goal is to choose an image that is safer to reuse in a document, slideshow, poster, or class activity.

Digital literacy focus

Search skills, image licensing, copyright awareness, and safe reuse of online content.

Example search term

Grand Canyon

Teaching point

Google says the Usage rights filter shows images with licence information attached, but users should still confirm the licence details on the original host website.

Open Google Images
Step 1

Open Google Images

Open your web browser and go to Google Images. In the search box, type the search words below and press Enter.

Grand Canyon

Checklist

Steps 2 and 3

Open Tools and Choose Usage Rights

After the image results appear, click Tools. Then click Usage rights and choose Creative Commons licences.

Do this slowly

What this means

Creative Commons images are often free to use, but they usually still require credit. Some may have conditions, such as non-commercial use only or do not modify.

Steps 4 and 5

Choose an Image and Check the Licence

Choose one Grand Canyon image that looks suitable for a classroom task or presentation. Do not download the first image automatically. First, check where the image comes from.

Look for these links

Final task

Write a Simple Image Credit

Create a simple image credit underneath the image. Use the format below.

Image: Grand Canyon Creator: [name of creator] Source: [website name] Licence: [Creative Commons licence type]

Your image credit

Example

Image: Grand Canyon. Creator: National Park Service. Source: Wikimedia Commons. Licence: Creative Commons Attribution.
